üîç Stock Analysis Framework

Before Investing in Any Stock, Check These Fundamentals:

  • P/E Ratio: Compare with industry average (Lower is generally better)
  • Revenue Growth: Look for consistent 15%+ annual growth
  • Profit Margins: Check if margins are improving
  • Debt-to-Equity: Lower is better (< 0.5 is ideal)
  • ROE (Return on Equity): Should be > 15%
  • Current Ratio: Should be > 1.5
  • Market Cap: Understand the company size
  • Sector Performance: Is the sector growing?
